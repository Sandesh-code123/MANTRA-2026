TrueCare Clinic has been serving the local community with honest and affordable health care for many years. What started as a single-room neighbourhood practice has grown into a multi-department clinic, but the promise to every patient has stayed the same: careful listening, a clear diagnosis, and treatment you can actually afford. Our team of qualified doctors covers everything from routine check ups to specialist care in cardiology, dental, eye care, pediatrics and general medicine, so most families never need to look elsewhere for everyday health needs.

Whether you are booking a first consultation, following up on an ongoing treatment, or bringing your child in for a routine check up, our reception and nursing staff are trained to make the visit as smooth as possible. We keep our appointment slots short so you are not left waiting, and our doctors take the time to explain your diagnosis in plain language rather than rushing you out the door. Have a look at our services below, meet our departments and doctors, and book an appointment whenever you need us.

About Us

Our Story

TrueCare Clinic started as a small neighbourhood clinic with one simple goal, to give people honest and affordable health care close to home. Over the years we have grown into a multi department clinic, but our approach has not changed. Every patient who walks through our door gets the same attention, respect and care.

Our Mission

We aim to provide quality health care that is easy to access, easy to understand and easy to afford. From routine check ups to specialist consultations, our doctors take time to listen before they prescribe.

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every patient with kindness and respect.

Honesty

Clear diagnosis and honest advice, always.

Excellence

We keep learning and upgrading our equipment and skills.

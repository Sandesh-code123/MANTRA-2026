Our Services

TrueCare Clinic offers a wide range of departments so your family can find the right care in one place. Have a look at what we offer below, then head to the appointment page to book a visit.

Cardiology

Heart check ups, ECG and consultation for cardiac conditions.

Dental Care

Cleaning, fillings, root canal and general dental treatment.

Eye Care

Vision testing, eye check ups and treatment for common eye problems.

Pediatrics

Health care for infants, children and teenagers.

General Medicine

Routine check ups, fever, infections and everyday health concerns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking in advance through our appointment page so we can plan your visit with the right doctor.

Is emergency care available at night?

Yes, our emergency desk is open 24 hours a day, every day of the week.

Do you treat children as well as adults?

Yes, our pediatrics department is dedicated to children while our other departments serve patients of all ages.
